## Quick facts
- Brand: Kardeşler Tekstil A.Ş. — corporate workwear manufacturer, est. 1982, Pendik/İstanbul
- Audience: Turkish B2B (kurumsal firmalar). Copy should be written in Turkish, `siz` form, no emoji.
- Primary channel: WhatsApp (`0216 396 19 88`). CTAs funnel to `Whatsapp'tan Teklif Al`.
- Palette: Workshop Navy `#0E2A44` primary · Hi-Vis Orange `#E6662A` accent · Bolt Red `#B92133` support · Cream `#F3EEE4` surface · Ink `#111418` text.
- Type: Archivo Black (display, UPPERCASE) + Inter Tight (body/UI). Both Google Fonts.
- Corner radii: small (2–8px). No pill buttons. No gradient backgrounds. Shadows sparingly.
- Icons: inline Lucide paths (see `ui_kits/website/Primitives.jsx` for set).

## ⚠️ Proposed, not approved
Nothing in this system was extracted from an existing brand book — the live site is a bare WordPress install. Treat the palette, type, logo, and iconography as proposals. Flag substitutions when shipping.
